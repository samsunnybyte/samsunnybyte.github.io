Meet Our Lead Abatement Experts

At Lead Free Homes LA, we are committed to making homes safer by eliminating lead-paint hazards, and our dedicated lead abatement technicians play a crucial role in making that happen. These highly trained professionals are experts in identifying and removing lead-paint risks safely, ensuring your home is a healthy environment for your loved ones. In the videos below, meet some of the trusted experts who may be part of your home's transformation, working with care and precision to create healthier living spaces for all.


Shi’Ann
Making a difference in the Community

Shi’Ann is one of our lead abatement technicians and is proud to protect families from lead paint hazards. Watch as she shares how her work helps families live in safer homes, free from dangerous lead paint.
